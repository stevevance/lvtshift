Chicago land value tax — by community area

Median parcel tax change and aggregate net change by property category, for each of Chicago's 77 community areas, under the revenue-neutral 2.5:1 split-rate and 100% building-abatement reforms (market-value base, TY2024 city levy). Tax changes are determined city-wide; each view is the slice of parcels whose centroid falls in the selected area. Positive = pays more.
