Cook County: a single county-wide land value tax (C1)

Modeled impact of replacing the entire Cook County property tax (every taxing body combined, ~$19.2B for tax year 2024) with one revenue-neutral land shift across the whole county. Current tax = each parcel's gross equalized value × its tax code's composite rate (Cook County Clerk). Two reforms: a 2.5:1 split-rate (land taxed 2.5× buildings, the Illinois classification cap) and a 100% building abatement (pure land tax). Tax changes are solved county-wide; each view is the slice of parcels in the selected assessment township. Positive = pays more.
